Opt-Out Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Your Right to Opt Out

You have the right to stop receiving notifications from Linear Digest at any time.

How to Opt Out

SMS Notifications

Reply STOP to any SMS message from Linear Digest to immediately unsubscribe from text notifications.

You can also contact the system administrator to disable SMS notifications for your account.

Email Notifications

Contact the system administrator to disable email notifications for your account.

Complete Removal

To be completely removed from Linear Digest, contact the system administrator. Your account will be deactivated and your personal information will be deleted.

What Happens After Opting Out

  • You will no longer receive digest notifications via the opted-out channel
  • Your Linear issues will no longer be queried for digest purposes
  • You can re-subscribe at any time by contacting the administrator

Contact

To opt out or for any questions, contact the system administrator.
