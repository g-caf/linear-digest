Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Overview

Linear Digest is an internal productivity tool that sends daily digest notifications about Linear issues to team members. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Name: Used to personalize notifications
  • Email address: Used to look up your Linear issues and send email digests
  • Phone number: Used to send SMS digest notifications

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Retrieve your assigned issues from Linear
  • Send you daily digest notifications via email and/or SMS

Data Storage

Your contact information is stored securely in our database. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., Twilio for SMS, Resend for email).

Data Retention

Your information is retained while you are an active user of the service. You may request deletion at any time by contacting the administrator.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Linear Digest:

  • Linear: To retrieve issue data
  • Twilio: To send SMS notifications
  • Resend: To send email notifications

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request data deletion, contact the system administrator.
